Adding and Playing Games

To add games, click on the 'Stash' button in the top left (next to the title) to open the Stash of games.

Then click CTRL+F and type in any game name of your choice, or find one in the list.

Download the game by clicking on the 'Download' icon (after you click the link beside the game name).

Then navigate back to the main website and click 'Browse' under 'Add Local Game'.

After you select a game, input the game title of choice and then click 'Add Game'.

You can now click on the game in the sidebar and play it.

If you'd like to make the game full-screen, click the 'Cloak' button in the top right.

Progress saves automatically, but the 'backup' and 'restore' features are there

as a failsafe. (If you plan to clear your computer's cache, use this!)

-

How to Remove Games

Click the red 'Remove Game' button.

It'll turn orange, and you can then click on a game.

A pop-up will ask if you want to delete the game.

If you select 'OK', the game will be deleted from your computer.

You can always add it back using the aforementioned steps to add games.
